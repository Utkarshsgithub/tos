interactive TED Talk presentation

These 1 hour long sessions are ideal for a sales conference type event. Punchy and to the point, our variety of interactive TED Talks will introduce your delegates to the mental models contained within the actors toolbox.

  • Secrets is a session tailored to exposing your people to a selection of processes actors regularly use to hone their skills. Focused on highlighting the commonalities between Acting and Sales exposure to these actors tools will give the delegates a few more strings to their bow.
  • Status is omni-present in the acting world. Recognizing and harnessing the power of status will give your people a whole new string to their bows when interacting with clients and prospective leads.
  • Story is the bread and butter of anyone in a sales role is their ability so spin a good yarn. This session is designed to break down the elements of a good story and give the delegates a plethora of different processes that can only add depth to their storytelling skills.
