The brain child of award-winning actor Niall Costigan and award-winning entrepreneur Hugo Pickford Wardle: Theatre of Sales is a forum designed for salespeople to benefit from the mindset shift and exposure to the mental models contained in the actors’ toolbox.

TOS started with trust

Niall and Hugo met many moons ago in Liverpool at LIPA (The Paul McCartney fame school!) Although majoring in different fields, Niall in Acting and Hugo in Management, they very soon crossed paths. Both a little eccentric in their own way Hugo and Niall’s brainstorming sessions were regularly awash with many a whacky scheme. Niall is convinced they came up with the selfie-stick…! Upon graduating they started a theatre company and took Niall’s production of Macbeth to the Liverpool Everyman and then on to Scarborough and NSDF festival where it won the Best Play award.

Post NSDF their respective careers flourished, Niall carved a path out for himself in the acting business and Hugo an entrepreneur in the business community, taking full advantage of the “performing” background he experienced at Uni. The famous brainstorming sessions became more sporadic as “Life and Kids” came along but they kept on simmering in the background and over 20 years after they first met a “lightbulb” moment happened…

Niall had spent years flirting with the corporate sector in between acting jobs and had always been intrigued by the parallels between the Sales and the Acting world. Niall came to realise early on in his career how transferable his skill set as an actor was to the Sales world. The training he received at drama school and consequential professional nous garnered in rehearsal rooms and on set always gave him that extra boost when confronted with hunting, closing or account development. Niall mentioned this to Hugo

“Do you know we do the same thing?” Hugo said “we are both actors.

“With our combined knowledge of the Acting and Business community, we could create an acting schools for sales reps?” continued Niall.

“Exactly” Hugo retorted with a smile…

Thus “Theatre of Sales” was born.

about theatre of sales

Theatre of sales was always about motivating and supporting ambitious individuals to realise their potential in their workplace. We offer services that allows the individual to see themselves as actors and apply this to their sales activites. Theatre of Sales is led by talented award-winning actor Niall Costigan and award-winning entrepreneur Hugo Pickford Wardle.
